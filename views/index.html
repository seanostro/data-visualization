By Is Angieri

Mar. 31, 2016 15:00 PM PDT

Mentions Tech, Biotech

Tech and Biotech IPOs that have taken place between 1980 and 2014 show macro trends of declining profitability and an increased number of IPOs.

The annual data suggest that while IPOs in other industries have remained largely stable, the percentage of profitable Tech and Biotech IPOs has decreased over the past thirty-four years.

Top Ridesharing Apps Marketshare across U.S., India, and Asia

By Is Angieri

Mar. 31, 2016 15:00 PM PDT

Mentions Uber, Lyft and 7 others

Monthly data from January to May shows Uber dominating Lyft in marketshare in the U.S., while making steady gains alongside competitor TaxiForSure on market leader, Ola cabs, in India. In Asia's market, Uber and GrabTaxi installbases show positive trends, while Easy Taxi's foothold in the market declines.

The monthly data suggest that while Uber dominates the U.S. rideshare market, competitors in India and Asia continue to lead the market by more than double the percentage of Uber's marketshare. Marketshare is measured as a percentage of installbase across tens of millions of Android phones.
